## What changed in V3

- The large pasted logo treatment was removed from the hero.
- The hero now uses a custom abstract brand device inspired by giving, continuity, growth, and impact.
- The actual Dar Al Ber logo is used more respectfully in the header, identity card, and footer.
- Arabic and English versions are included as two clean static pages.
- Messaging is presented directly as a demo website, without over-emphasizing “what we changed.”
- Official association facts, news, policies, contact information, and help-request logic are integrated into the website language.
- The slogan and terminology are preserved:
  - من التبرع إلى الأثر
  - رحلة التبرع الذكية
  - مركز الثقة والسياسات
  - صافي الأثر الوقفي
  - بوابة المتبرع
  - لوحة الأثر
